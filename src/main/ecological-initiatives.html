What are Tokenized Ecological Initiatives?

Unique NFTs that serve as proof of support

NFTs as Immutable Records

We authenticate ecological initiatives by tokenizing applicable data into an NFT called a Tokenized Ecological Initiative (TEI), backed by the appreciable value of an initiative. These TEIs are never sold, and act as a public ledger of our work.

Asset-Based Collectibles

We then issue tradable NFTs that are based on the valuation of the original TEI (NFTEIs) and the natural assets they protect. This allows users to purchase exclusive, tangible collectibles which enable us to fund our ecological mission.

The Value
of Nature

NFTEI use-cases go beyond fundraising. Features include metaverse and game add-ons, AR/VR functionality, evolving imagery, and more. They also enable the resale of this proof of support - and further fundraising through royalties.
