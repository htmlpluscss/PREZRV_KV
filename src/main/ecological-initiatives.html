Tokenized Ecological Initiatives

Unique NFTs that serve as proof of support

NFTs as Immutable
Records

We authenticate ecological initiatives by turning documentation and data into an NFT called a Tokenized Ecological Initiative (TEI), backed by the proof of our work. These TEIs are never sold, and act as a public ledger of our efforts.

Asset-Based
Collectibles

We then issue tradable NFTs that are based on the valuation of the original TEI (NFTEIs) and the natural assets they protect. This lets users own tangible, exclusive collectibles which allow them to directly assist in our ecological mission.

The Value
of Nature

NFTEIs are more personal and proactive than traditional carbon markets. They can be utilized within PREZRV, and across the metaverse. The resale of this proof-of-support can also bolster our fundraising through royalties.
