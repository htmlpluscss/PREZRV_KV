Regenerative Contribution Cycle

Our structure not only regenerates the costs our organization incurs from performing ecological initatives - it also allows supporters to regenerate the inital cost they incurred from their contribution

  • After community voting and approval,
    we can use the funds collected
    in our treasury to purchase a plot
    of land, in order to establish a brand
    new nature preserve.
  • We tokenize the contract of sale
    for the land, and record the verifiable valuation and cost of this particular initiative on the blockchain
    for immutable transparency.
  • Once the NFTEIs are sold, the cost
    of the initiative is covered. This allows
    us to perpetually support ecological initatives of all types from reforestation, bioremediation, environmental research, and beyond.
  • Issued NFTEIs are always based
    on the value of a TEI. If the value
    of purchased land in the TEI is $100,000, we could issue 100 NFTEIs at a price
    of $1,000. These NFTEIs can be easily resold by supporters.
